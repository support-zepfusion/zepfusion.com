Farmer lifecycle

From plot to payment

Simple for farmers on a phone; serious for your control tower—each stage can roll out when data and partners are ready.

1

Onboard & map

Identity, plots, and consents—lightweight first, richer land context as you scale.

2

Plan & sow

AI-assisted crop calendar and input plan—aligned with weather and market signals you trust.

3

Monitor & log

Field events, inputs, and alerts—feeding a traceable batch story for certification and buyers.

4

Harvest & grade

Quality checks and batching—ready for QR labels and export paperwork flows.

5

Sell & settle

Direct listings, buyer matching, delivery proof, and escrow-style release when rules are met.

6

Finance & repeat

Credit and insurance partners plug in when history exists—season two gets smarter than season one.

Trust & privacy

Data farmers and buyers can trust

Farmer data is sensitive—consent, retention, and programme rules come first. On-chain elements are for agreed proofs and handoffs, not for dumping PII on a public ledger.

When you need stronger assurance for exporters or financiers, we align anchoring, identity, and documents to their expectations—step by step.

Your programme, your brand

Tenants, FPOs, and enterprises keep their own policies and UX—farmers see your programme, not a generic app shell. Roles from field agent to buyer stay scoped.
