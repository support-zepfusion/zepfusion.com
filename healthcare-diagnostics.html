Patient journey

From booking to report in hand

Clear for patients and traceable for your team—so everyone knows what happens next.

1

Book & pay

Patient books online or at the desk; tests and pricing are confirmed and the lab workflow starts.

2

Sample collection

Samples are registered and tracked—barcodes or labels tie each tube to the right patient and order.

3

Results

Technicians and instruments feed results into one place; reviewers see status at a glance.

4

Report ready

Final PDF and structured details are released to patients and referrers in the channels you choose.

5

Optional seal

When you need it, reports can carry a digital seal so third parties can confirm authenticity.

6

Easy check

A QR code on the report lets patients verify it in seconds—issuer, date, and whether an updated version exists.

Trust & privacy

Confidence in every report

Patients and referrers should know your report is genuine. When corrections are needed, they should be handled clearly—without confusion or dispute.

If you later need stronger proof between organisations—hospitals, insurers, or regulators—we can grow with you step by step.

Your network, your brand

Each chain or group keeps its own data and branding—your patients never see another lab’s records. Access is limited by role: front desk, lab, clinician, admin—so people see only what they need.
